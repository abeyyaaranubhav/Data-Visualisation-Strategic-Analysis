# PowerBI-project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the **Tata Group Data Visualisation Virtual Experience** via Forage. The goal was to transform raw retail data into actionable insights for the CEO and CMO to guide a global expansion strategy.

The project involved data cleaning, quantitative analysis, and creating high-impact visuals in **Power BI** to identify revenue trends, top-performing markets, and growth opportunities.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Skills Demonstrated
* **Data Cleaning & Transformation:** Utilized Power Query to filter records, handle null values, and remove negative quantities/unit prices to ensure data integrity.
* **DAX & Calculated Measures:** Created custom columns for Revenue and implemented time-series analysis.
* **Advanced Data Visualisation:** Developed executive-level dashboards including:
    * Time-series Line Charts for seasonality analysis.
    * Top-N Clustered Bar Charts for market ranking.
    * Geospatial Mapping for global demand identification.
* **Stakeholder Communication:** Prepared a professional video presentation translating technical data into strategic business recommendations for C-suite executives.

---

## 📊 Key Business Questions Addressed
1.  **Revenue Trends:** What was the monthly revenue performance for 2011? (Identified significant Q4 seasonality).
2.  **Top Markets:** Which 10 countries (excluding the UK) generated the highest revenue and volume?
3.  **Customer Value:** Who are the top 10 customers by revenue to target for retention programs?
4.  **Expansion Strategy:** Where are the global "hotspots" for demand that warrant new localized operations?

---

## 📂 Project Structure
* `data/`: Contains the raw and cleaned datasets.
* `visuals/`: Power BI (.pbix) files and dashboard screenshots.
* `scripts/`: Analysis notes and presentation script.
* `README.md`: Project documentation.

---

## 🚀 Key Insights & Recommendations
* **Seasonality:** Revenue peaks in late Q3 and throughout Q4. Supply chain readiness should be prioritized by August.
* **Regional Focus:** The Netherlands and Ireland are the strongest expansion candidates with high volume-to-revenue correlation.
* **Customer Strategy:** A small percentage of customers drive a majority of revenue; a "VIP" corporate account program is recommended for the expansion phase.
