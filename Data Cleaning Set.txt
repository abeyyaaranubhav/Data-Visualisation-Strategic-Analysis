DATA CLEANING & TRANSFORMATION LOG
----------------------------------
Tool Used: Power BI (Power Query / M-Engine)

The following steps were performed to ensure data integrity and accuracy 
before creating visualizations for the CEO and CMO:

1. RECORD FILTERING (Quantity):
   - Constraint: Quantity must be greater than or equal to 1.
   - Logic: Removed all records with negative values to exclude 
     returns, cancellations, and data entry errors from the sales analysis.

2. RECORD FILTERING (Unit Price):
   - Constraint: Unit Price must be greater than or equal to 0.
   - Logic: Filtered out invalid pricing data to ensure revenue 
     calculations remain accurate.

3. CUSTOM CALCULATIONS (Revenue):
   - Created a new calculated column [Revenue].
   - Formula: [Quantity] * [UnitPrice]
   - Purpose: To provide a primary KPI for trend analysis and 
     ranking visuals.

4. DATA TYPING:
   - InvoiceDate: Converted to 'Date' format for time-series compatibility.
   - Revenue: Formatted as 'Currency' (2 decimal places).
   - Quantity: Formatted as 'Whole Number'.

5. GEOSPATIAL STANDARDIZATION:
   - Verified 'Country' names to ensure 100% mapping accuracy for 
     the Global Demand Map visual.
